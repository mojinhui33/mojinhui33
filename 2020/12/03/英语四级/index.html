英语四级

发表于 2020-12-03 分类于 四级

作文翻译

一般16-18行

被动
  • 很多人认为合作比个人能力更重要

Cooperation is claimed to be more crucial than personal ability.

  • 人们普遍认为网购已经成为一种流行趋势。

Online shopping is argued to be a popular trend among the young and the old.

  • 在过去几十年里，政府采取了各种措施防止灾害发生

Various measures, in the past decades, have been taken to prevent disasters.

  • 最近，中国科学院出版了关于其最新科学发现与未来一年展望的年度系列报告

An report about its latest scientific findings was recently published by Chinese Academy of Science

只要听到人或人称代词作主语，都可以考虑写成被动

并列句

写作和翻译的时候，只要上下有逻辑关系，就一定要用逻辑关系次，（在语法中有出现）

每个背两个

  • 因果关系

    • 表原因：because, int that, as, since | because of = be responsible for = due to = owing to = thanks to
    • 表结果：so = hence = therefore = thereby = thus = consequently = in consequence = as a result/consequence

  • 表示对比

    • 相似性的对比=and：likewise = similarly = equally = simultaneously = at the same time = in the meanwhile
    • 差异性的对比=but：but, by contrast = conversely = alternatively = on the contrary = on the other hand = otherwise = while = whereas

  • 表示递进

    • besides = also = in addition = moreover = furthermore = apart/aside from to put it another way = put another way = in other words

  • 表示让步

    • despite = in spite of = although = though = as = while = even (if) = much as 虽然
    • certainly = of course = indeed = it is true that 的确
    • undoubtedly = there is no doubt that …毫无疑问的是
    • it may be true that …或许真实的是…
    • there is no denying that … 不可否认的是..
    • in effect / fact/reality = indeed = virtually 事实上，的确

  • 表示目的

    • to = in order to = so as to

  • 表示引出新对象（文章开头用）

    • about = concerning = regarding = with regard to = as for/ to = in respect of 关于
    • in terms of 就…而论，在…方面
    • when it comes to .. 当提到…时

  • 表示总结（一定用的到，在文章最后一段，只需一个）

    • On the whole = all in all = overall = in conclusion/ summary/ general = generally = as a whole = mainly = substantially = to sum up = conclude = come to the conclusion 总的来说
    • in short/ brief = in a word 简而言之
    • in any case = at any rate = anyway 总之，无论如何

例子：

  • 在过去一千年里，乌镇的水系和生活方式并未经历多少变化，是一座展现古文明的博物馆

In the past thousand years, the water system and the life way of Wuzhen have not gone through (经历）many changes. Therefore, it now is the museum to show ancient civilization

  • 在山东潍坊市，风筝不仅仅是玩具，而且还是这座城市文化的标志

In Werfang, Shandong, kites（要复数） are not only for enertainment but also symbolizes the culture of the city

  • 这些资金用于改善教学设施、购买书籍，使16万多所中小学收益。资金还用于购置音乐和绘画器材。现在农村和山区的儿童可以与沿海城市的儿童一样上音乐和绘画课

These funds are used to improve educational facilities, purchase books and more than 160 thousands primary schools and middle schools benefit from it. In addition, the fund is to be used for purchasing music and painting facilities. As a consequence, nowadays the children in the rural areas can have the music and painting classes as the same as the children in the coastal cities do

  • 由于这个传统，许多年轻人婚后继续与父母同住。今天，这个传统正在改变。随着住房条件的改善，越来越多年轻夫妇选择与父母分开住。 但他们之间的联系依然很密切。 许多老年人仍然帮着照看孙辈。 年轻夫妇也抽时间探望父母， 特别是在春节和中秋节等重要节日

As a result of this tradition, many young people continue to live with their parents after marriage. by contrast, today, the tradition is changing. With the improvement of housing conditions, an increasing number of young couples choose to live apart from their parents. Nevertheless, the connection between them remains strong. Many old people still have to look after their grandchildren. At the same time, young couples also get around to visit their parents, especially during the holidays, such as the spring festival and Midautumn Festival.

复合句

  1. 主语从句：就是把主句放到句末，加it来做形式主语

    • 大学教育极大地促进了社会的进步是显而易见的

      It is beyond dispute that university education promotes the progress of our society obviously.

  1. 同位语和同位语从句（名词，对这个名词的解释，句子）就记名词同位语即可

    • Garbage sorting, a necessary behavior to control pollution, proves quite crucial throughout China.
    • Hunting for jobs, a hot topic among undergraduates, has become the public concern recently.
    • Optimism, an active mentality, palys a key role in the general routine of everyday living.

  2. 定于和定语从句

    • i love this kind girl（名词作定语）
    • i love this girl of kindness.（介词短语作定语）
    • i love the girl as kind as an angel（非谓语动词作定语）
    • i love the girl being as kind as an angel（从句作定语）
    • i love the girl girl who keeps so kind

例子：

  • 中国结最初是由手工艺人发明的，巾帼数百年不断的改进，已经成为一种优雅多彩的艺术和工艺

The Chines knot was originally invented by the craftsmen, which has become a kind of elegant and colorful arts and crafts after hundreds of years of continuous improvement.

  • 乌镇是浙江的一座古老水镇，坐落在京杭大运河畔。这是一处迷人的地方，有许多古桥、中式旅店和餐馆

Wuzhen, which is an ancient water town of Zhejiang province, locates near the Beijing-Hangzhou Grand Canal, which is really a charming place with many ancient bridges, Chinese stylish hotels and restaurants there.

  • 剪纸是中国最为流行的传统民间艺术形式之一。中国剪纸有一千五百多年的历史，在明朝和清朝时期特别流行(主语一致都可以写成定语从句）

Paper cutting is one of the most popular arts in China which has a history of 1.5 thound years and was quite famous in the Ming and Qing dynasties.

  1. 状语和状语从句（时间、地点、原因、目的、结果、条件、让步、方式（by）、比较）
  • I love my girlfriend.后面加状语（上面的）
  • keeping pets can cultivate the love heart of children | although they something bring much trouble for their owners.（后面这一部分为状语）
    • or because taking care of those little animals needs patience and care.
    • or when there exists only a child in one family

作文中任何一句话都可以加一个状语的成分，把句子拉长

三大经典句型（选修）

  1. 强调句型

Nothing is more important than self-reliance in a competitive internationa environment

再国际竞争环境下，没有什么比自立更重要了

强调什么就把他放入 it is …. that….

  • 就是因为你，我没有通过六级考试

It is because of you that I failed in CET-6

  1. 虚拟语气

If you are a tear in my eyes, I will never shed it.

把条件状语从句变成虚拟语气，只需要把已经写好的条件状语从句变成他的过去式就行了，并且再虚拟语气中，be动词的过去式只有were 没有 was

If you were a tear in my eyes, I would never shed it.

  1. 倒装（最高级，基础差不用）

  2. 否定词放在句首用倒装

  • 我从没这样开心过

Never have i been so happy.

  • 在任何情况下我们绝不能忽略…的重要性/严重性

Under no circumstances can we fail to pour attention into … （gravity/ severity）

  1. 含有so…that结构的句子，将so引导的部分谓语句首
  2. only引出的状语位于句首时
  • 只有以这种方式，我们才能解决这个问题

Only in this way can we settle the issue/relieve the phenomenon.

句子的改写和扩写

  1. 比如 我绝不嫁给你

    • I will never marry you
    • You are the last man I want to marry.（定语从句）
    • I will not marry you unless the sun rises in the west（状语从句）

  2. 自行车很重要

    • Bicycles are important.
    • Humans cannot live without bicycles

句子扩写：（同位语/定于/状语），前两者只能加在名词后面

  • I love xixi.

I, a university student from beijing, love xixi who is the most graceful and elegant English teacher I have ever seen

  • Smile makes people love you.

Smile, the best means for communicating, makes people who may look serious and distant love at first sight.

  • Pollution keeps exeedingly serous.

Pollution, a global issue which threatens almost every one, keeps exceedingly serous with the rapid urbanization.

书信：

第一段，（比较熟的人）问候语加写信目的，（不太熟的人）自我介绍加写信目的

+, the letter, to be honest, is to 文中给出内容 to you.

例如：

Dear friend,

I am delighted to know that you have a strong desire to learn Chinese in recent days.therefore, the letter, to be honest, is to recommend a city to you

中间一段
Firstly,………Besides,……..

Wen the idea occurs to me, the first city flashing in my mind is Bejing, the capital of China.Firstly,there exists language environment which plays a key role in the process of language learning. As a result, as a modern city, it provides an excellent language environment for language learners, Besides, in one of the biggest citties, people are open-minded, hospitable, and inclusive. You will soon find that you can make friends with them quickly

最后一段：（熟人）期待回信+祝福语 （生人）表示感谢+期待回信

Finally, I hope that you could take my suggestions into serious consideration, and I am looking forward to your reply at your earliest convenience.Good luck to you with all aspects of life.

Yours sincerely.

Li Ming

除了书信，其他都是论说文

第一段，阐述现象+个人观点

The use of translation apps is becoming increasingly popular, which has aroused a long-term discussion among the public（非限制性定语从句，模版）. From my perspective（模版）, whether translation app is practical depends on the need of different individuals.

前面还可以加

In light of the rapid improvement of science and technology,………（随着科学和技术进步）

第二段，原因分析

On one hand,…..Besides,….On the other hand…..

The main reason is…….Then more importantly,……

Quite a few elements can account for my perspective（总述模版）. On one hand, there is no doubt（显而易见） that the use of translation app could be convenient and efficient when you read certain foreign materials including some professional papers. Besides, it is when people go abroad where they cannot speak native languages that using translation apps could make them communicate with natives more fluently. On the other hand, translation apps, after all, are perated by machines. thus, it is inevitable that some translation goes against original expression, and even worse, some grammar faults could be found in the translation.

第三段，总结+措施

As a result,(模板）Just as a coin has two sides, （有好有坏模版）translation apps are no exception. That is to say, when we download a translation app, we should distinguish its good from bad and use it within reason. Only in this way can we make translation apps exert a positive effect on our study and career.

模版

*议论文8

第一段引出主题的句子：

  1. Recently the issue/problem of …has been in the limelight（处于关注的中心）/brought into focus/brought to public attentive（留意的，关心的）concern…

  2. Recently with the development of science and technology, the phenomenon of … has aroused wide concern.

第一段表示个人观点的表达：

In my opinion

第二段分析原因的总起句：

As we know, recognizing a problem is the first step in finding a solution.

两个或三个原因分析，用定于状语拉长

第三段总结措施：

措施 我们/政府/家长/学校，一个采取就行
